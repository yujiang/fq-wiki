# 《风起江湖》角色立绘 Prompt 模板

> 本文档用于统一《风起江湖》项目中角色立绘的 AI 绘制风格，目标是：
>
> **人物生动、气质鲜明、具有江湖画卷感，而非写实摄影感。**
>
> 适用于：角色立绘、设定集插图、Wiki 展示图、剧情关键插画。

---

## 一、通用总原则（所有角色通用）

在每一条 Prompt 中，**建议保留以下基础描述**，作为风格锚点：

```text
fantasy character illustration,
wuxia / xianxia inspired,
elegant and expressive,
anime-inspired painterly style,
designed as a game character portrait
```

### 风格说明
- **illustration / painterly**：确保是插画而非照片
- **character portrait**：人物优先，背景从属
- **expressive**：强调情绪与气质，而非材质真实性

---

## 二、男性侠客立绘 Prompt（主角 / 剑客 / 行走江湖者）

### 角色气质关键词
- 内敛
- 坚定
- 英气而不张扬
- 有故事感

### Prompt 模板

```text
A highly detailed fantasy wuxia male character illustration,
calm and confident demeanor,
sharp yet gentle eyes with subtle highlights,
slightly messy flowing hair with natural motion,
refined traditional martial attire with ornamental details,
elegant and restrained posture,
soft and cinematic illustrative lighting,
anime-inspired painterly style,
designed as a game character portrait,
dark and simple background to emphasize the character
```

### 适用角色示例
- 主角
- 徐剑
- 偏“守、忍、义”的男性人物

---

## 三、女性侠客立绘 Prompt（段思思 / 乐小钗 / 江湖女性）

### 角色气质关键词
- 美而不媚
- 理智、坚韧
- 气质胜过外形

### Prompt 模板（重点推荐）

```text
A beautiful fantasy wuxia female character illustration,
elegant and graceful,
expressive eyes with intelligence and calm determination,
soft facial features with a gentle smile,
flowing hair with delicate movement,
refined traditional outfit with intricate ornaments,
soft and beautiful illustrative lighting,
anime-inspired painterly style,
designed as a game character portrait,
dark or neutral background to enhance character presence
```

### 适用角色示例
- 段思思（高度适配）
- 乐小钗
- 李师师（青年期）
- 苏枕雪

---

## 四、少年 / 少女立绘 Prompt（新生代角色）

> ⚠️ 此类角色最容易被画“过熟”，请谨慎使用成熟相关词汇。

### 角色气质关键词
- 清澈
- 青涩
- 未来感

### Prompt 模板

```text
A youthful fantasy wuxia character illustration,
bright and clear eyes full of curiosity,
slightly innocent and lively expression,
soft facial features with youthful charm,
light and flowing hair,
simple yet elegant traditional outfit,
gentle and warm illustrative lighting,
anime-inspired painterly style,
designed as a game character portrait,
clean and minimal background
```

### 避免使用的词汇
- mature
- seductive
- strong facial structure

---

## 五、成熟女性立绘 Prompt（极重要、极高价值）

> 成熟 ≠ 年老，而是**被时间打磨后的从容与温柔**。

### 角色气质关键词
- 温柔
- 从容
- 情绪深度
- 压得住场

### Prompt 模板（收藏级）

```text
A mature and elegant fantasy wuxia woman illustration,
gentle yet confident expression,
warm and expressive eyes with emotional depth,
soft and refined facial features,
flowing hair with graceful movement,
highly refined traditional attire with exquisite ornaments,
beautiful and soft illustrative lighting,
anime-inspired painterly style,
designed as a game character portrait,
dark and subtle background to emphasize elegance
```

### 适用角色示例
- 白桦
- 阮青丝
- 成熟线李师师
- 经历风雨的江湖女性角色

---

## 六、防止“精致但僵硬”的保险语句（强烈推荐）

当画面开始变得写实但缺乏灵魂时，可追加：

```text
focus on character charm and emotional presence rather than realism
```

---

## 七、提词顺序黄金法则（建议牢记）

```text
身份 → 气质 → 眼神 → 动势 → 服饰 → 光影 → 风格 → 用途
```

遵循此顺序，可最大程度避免风格跑偏。
